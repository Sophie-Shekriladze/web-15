We have sought to bring our passion for fashion to the world from the start. Present in more than 115 markets, "FOR" adapts to each country, with a complete ecosystem of international channels and partners and a network of more than 2,500 physical points of sale and a powerful online platform.

With roots in Barcelona, one of the cradles of the textile industry, "FOR" has been looking to the future and inspiring the world with its passion for fashion and lifestyle for the past four decades. Since its inception, "FOR" has placed creativity and design at the centre of all its decisions, with garments that seek to stand out with their very own identity.
